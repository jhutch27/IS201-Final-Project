How to play

Pick a difficulty, enter a number in the current range, and press Guess. The game will tell you if your guess is too high or too low and how close you are.

Choose difficulty

The harder the mode, the larger the number range and the fewer attempts you get.

  • Easy: numbers 1 to 50 with plenty of room to narrow it down.
  • Medium: numbers 1 to 100 for a balanced challenge.
  • Hard: numbers 1 to 250 with fewer chances and trickier jumps.
